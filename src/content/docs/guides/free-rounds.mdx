Free rounds (also called free spins) let a player spin a game without staking their own cash — the stake is funded by a grant you or the provider issued, and any winnings are credited to the player. The Aggregator delivers free-round wallet events to your existing callback endpoint. **There is no new endpoint and no new action to implement** — a free round arrives as a normal `bet` / `win` callback with one extra signal: `is_free: true`.

If you have not yet integrated the standard wallet callback, read [Wallet callbacks](/guides/wallet-callbacks/) first. This guide only describes what is *different* for free rounds.

:::caution[The one rule that matters most]
**Never debit the player's real-money balance for a `bet` callback where `is_free` is `true`.** The stake is covered by the free-round grant, not the player's wallet. The `amount` on a free bet is the *nominal* stake (for your reporting / GGR), **not** a real-money charge. A naive integration that debits it will silently take real money from players on every free spin.
:::

## What changes for a free round

A free-round callback is the standard forward payload (see [Wallet callbacks → Forward Payload](/guides/wallet-callbacks/#forward-payload)) plus these fields:

| Field | Type | Description |
|-------|------|-------------|
| `is_free` | `boolean` | `true` when this callback belongs to a free round. **This is the field you branch on.** Absent or `false` on ordinary cash play. |
| `free_round_grant_id` | `string` | Identifies the free-round grant this spin belongs to. Use it to group a campaign's activity. May be an empty string if the grant could not be resolved (e.g. an operator-issued voucher, or a race) — the spin still settles, just unlinked. |
| `free_round_kind` | `string \| null` | Provider-defined label for the kind of free round. Informational; safe to ignore. |

`action`, `transaction_type`, `transaction_id`, `amount`, `currency`, `player_id`, `provider_code`, and `session_id` carry exactly the same meaning and units as on a normal callback — `amount` and `balance` are **integers in minor units** ([details](/guides/wallet-callbacks/#body)). Branch on `transaction_type` and `is_free`, never on `action`.

## Balance handling

| Callback | `is_free` | What to do |
|----------|-----------|------------|
| `bet` | `true` | **Do not touch the real-money balance.** The grant funds the stake. Record the spin for reporting if you wish; `amount` is the nominal stake, not a charge. |
| `win` | `true` | **Credit the player** by `amount`, exactly like a normal win. Optionally book it as bonus funds subject to your wagering / withdrawability rules (see below). |
| `bet` / `win` | absent / `false` | Ordinary cash play — debit / credit as documented in [Wallet callbacks](/guides/wallet-callbacks/#transaction-types). |

The net effect of a correctly handled free round is that the player risks nothing (free bet is not debited) and keeps the winnings (free win is credited).

```
// pseudo-code — the only change vs cash play is the is_free guard on bet
if (callback.transaction_type === "bet" && callback.is_free) {
  // grant-funded — DO NOT debit. Optionally log for reporting.
  balance = currentBalance(callback.player_id);
} else if (callback.transaction_type === "bet") {
  balance = debit(callback.player_id, callback.amount);   // normal cash bet
} else if (callback.transaction_type === "win") {
  balance = credit(callback.player_id, callback.amount);  // free or cash — credit either way
}
```

### Winnings and withdrawability

`is_free` exists so you can apply your own promotional accounting. Free-round winnings are commonly treated as **bonus funds** subject to a wagering requirement before withdrawal. Whether you credit them as cash or as a restricted bonus balance is entirely your decision — The Aggregator does not impose a model. If you do nothing special, crediting them as cash is valid and the player simply keeps the winnings.

## Provider differences — don't assume a bet always precedes a win

The exact callback sequence depends on the upstream provider. Your handler must cope with both shapes, which is why you branch on `is_free` per callback rather than assuming a `bet` always arrives before a `win`:

- **Per-spin providers (e.g. Apparat).** Each free spin produces a `bet` (`is_free: true`, nominal stake) and, on a winning spin, a `win` (`is_free: true`). You will see both legs.
- **Aggregate-settlement providers (e.g. TRUE LABS).** The whole batch of free rounds settles as a single `win` (`is_free: true`) carrying the total winnings, with `finished: true`. **No `bet` leg is forwarded** — the stakes never touch the wallet path at all. A zero-win batch still settles (you may receive a `win` with `amount: 0`).

Treat each callback independently by `transaction_id` and `is_free`. Do not require a matching free `bet` before accepting a free `win`.

## Example payloads

A free spin's bet leg (per-spin provider) — **record, do not debit**:

```json
{
  "action": "BET",
  "transaction_type": "bet",
  "is_free": true,
  "free_round_grant_id": "9f1c0b3a-2d4e-4a6b-8c0d-1e2f3a4b5c6d",
  "free_round_kind": "freespin",
  "transaction_id": "provider-unique-tx-id",
  "amount": 20,
  "currency": "EUR",
  "player_id": "player-123",
  "provider_code": "apparat",
  "session_id": "a1b2c3d4-e5f6-7890-abcd-ef1234567890"
}
```

The free win — **credit the player**:

```json
{
  "action": "WIN",
  "transaction_type": "win",
  "is_free": true,
  "free_round_grant_id": "9f1c0b3a-2d4e-4a6b-8c0d-1e2f3a4b5c6d",
  "free_round_kind": "freespin",
  "transaction_id": "freespin:9f1c0b3a-2d4e-4a6b-8c0d-1e2f3a4b5c6d",
  "amount": 450,
  "currency": "EUR",
  "player_id": "player-123",
  "provider_code": "truelabs",
  "finished": true,
  "session_id": "a1b2c3d4-e5f6-7890-abcd-ef1234567890"
}
```

Both are signed with your `callback_secret` exactly like any other callback — verify `X-SIGNATURE` against the raw body before processing ([Signature verification](/guides/signature-verification/)).

## Idempotency

No change from the standard contract: use `transaction_id` as your idempotency key and return the same response on a duplicate. The free `win` from an aggregate-settlement provider uses a stable `transaction_id` of the form `freespin:<grant_id>`, so a retried settlement is naturally de-duplicated. See [Idempotency](/guides/idempotency/).

## Go-Live Checklist — free rounds

Complete these in addition to the standard [wallet-callback go-live checklist](/guides/wallet-callbacks/#go-live-checklist).

- [ ] Your handler reads `is_free` on every callback and branches on it.
- [ ] A `bet` with `is_free: true` does **not** debit the player's real-money balance.
- [ ] A `win` with `is_free: true` credits the player (as cash, or as bonus funds per your promo rules).
- [ ] You do not require a preceding free `bet` before accepting a free `win` (aggregate-settlement providers send only the win).
- [ ] You handle a free `win` with `amount: 0` (a losing free-round batch still settles).
- [ ] Idempotency keys off `transaction_id` for free rounds too, including the `freespin:<grant_id>` form.
- [ ] You verified the signature on a free-round callback with a known body + secret pair.
